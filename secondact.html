Konakanchi Sai Sucharitha snips a play work

THE EXCHANGE

BY

ALTHEA THURSTON

JUDGE.

[_Shortly._]

You are.

RICH CITIZEN.

[_Languidly, between puffs of his cigarette._]

Well, Judge, life has become rather boresome, so I thought I would drop in and ask you to do me a small favor.

JUDGE.

[_Wearily._]

Yes? We--What is your grievance?
